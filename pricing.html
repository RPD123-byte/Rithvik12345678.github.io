    Less than 1 million dollars

    If you trust us to manage less than 1 million dollars of your assets than the fee will based on commission of 3% of your overall capital.

    For example, if you invest $500,000 you would get charged 3% and would pay $15,000 per year.

    There will also be additional performance fees. If you get a 50% or higher return on your money in one year then you will be charged an additional 3% on solely the profits.

    For example if you invest $500,000 and we make you an additional $250,000 in the span of one year. You will be charged an additional $7,500.
